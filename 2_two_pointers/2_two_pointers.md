# Two Pointers (NeetCode 150)

This folder contains problems related to the **two pointers** technique.  
The approach usually involves using two indices (start/end or slow/fast) to reduce time complexity from O(n²) → O(n).

## ✍️ Notes
- Core idea: use **two pointers** to avoid nested loops.
- Common setups:
    - **Left & right ends** (e.g., Container With Most Water).
    - **Slow & fast pointers** (e.g., Valid Palindrome).
- Often combined with **sorting** for 3Sum / 4Sum problems.
